"My, oh my! What we do get up to in San Francisco!"

After a fabulous, six-installment summer series of broadcasts on Candlewax Radio showcasing the very best classic jazz on vinyl, our own Mr. Sellgren (aka 'The Candle') is preparing a killer new series that will air here on Entourage beginning in November. Hold on to your hats: it's the Original Music Series, and it promises to be Very Tall indeed.

To inaugarate the Original Music Series in Herb Caen-mention-worthy style, we're planning a Live Round Up -complete with de rigueur filming- to celebrate the first broadcast.

For the event, head over to your corner City Car Share pod and borrow a convertible with a nice radio. That way you'll be able to be able to listen to the low-range FM broadcast while reaching the quick-moving drinks tray with ease.

Grab your spot somewhere along the curb of the cul-de-sac, and get ready for Sandy, our long-legged, dance-blading waitress in fluerescent hot pink to roll by. Yup, you twigged it! Sandy's getup is a nod to the color scheme of those crazy Asian Fan Dancers in the memorable Courtney video for Sex ('for my love there is no cure...') released in Japan oh those many years ago. Robbie, I hope you have your sequinned number at hand.

Plus you'll need the car to get to the film shoot at dawn along the cliffs at Point Lobos, up on the Headlands and down along the wharf -- but more on that later.
